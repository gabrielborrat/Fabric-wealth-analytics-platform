# Risk & Exposure Dashboard

## Objectives
This dashboard provides risk analysis and exposure monitoring capabilities.

## Pages
1. **Overview**: Portfolio risk summary and key risk metrics
2. **FX Risk Detail**: Foreign exchange exposure analysis

## Target Audience
- Risk managers
- Portfolio managers
- Compliance officers

## Key Metrics
- Portfolio risk metrics
- FX exposure by currency
- Value at Risk (VaR)
- Maximum drawdown
- Concentration risk
